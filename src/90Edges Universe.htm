    Today it is exactly six months since I decided to start working on 90Edges Universe. To this occasion we switched our web into a new web design (made by Jan Červený). Do you like it? I do!

    By the way, you can take a look on the screenshot of a new player-created content, a space station, made by Jaroslav Šmat. Jaroslav is one of our excited players/testers of internal alpha version.

    Progress

    Actual development (2nd quarter) has been very slow last three months because I focused on looking for new team members, sponsors and investors (see more info in following sections). Next three months (3rd quarter) will be more about intensive development again and also about marketing tasks. I guess you may be interested in a blog post related to our 1st quarter - if you missed it. Source code of our project is hosted in GitHub private repository. See the graphical representation of my contributions in last 6 months:

    Team

    We are a virtual team and we will, I hope, always be. I am working full-time on this game and all other team members are contractors from various cities round the world (our team members are currently located in 3 different countries in Europe and United States). From a small team made only by 2 developers we grew up during last quarter into team made by 7 developers (programming, music composer, web design, quality assurance, game design and two artists). Stay tuned, we will introduce you our new team members in following weeks!

    Funding - First and second quarter

    We have several FF Sponsors (FF stands for Friends and Family) and several FF Investors. All those sponsors and investors are currently willing to support 90Edges Universe on monthly basis as long as they are satisfied with direction and growth of our project. Dear friends and family members, thank you for your support!

    It seems, at this moment, we have a clear way how to finance at least next 18 months of development.

    Introducing Main Features of 90Edges Universe

    Before I write something about main features I would like to share a new space station made by Michal Hantl in 90Edges Universe 0.0.6-alpha. It is based on his space station we shared on Twitter few days ago. Click on the screenshot to see it in HD.

    Main features:

    • Explore - On your space station will be available a space telescope which can be used for observation of distant galaxies and other outer space objects. When you find nice place (nice galaxy or something) you may jump (with “hyperspace” jump drive - FTL) with whole space station deep into unknown universe close to the object you just found. It looks like we will be able to provide such a big zoom that you may see one single star in distance of 14 billion parsecs (distance which 
      represent the radius of the visible universe - in real universe we live in). There will be also possibility to find and explore other player’s space stations (inside). 
    • Build - You may build space station and then build structures and puzzles (in corridors) inside your space station. There will be possibility to design and build space stations for other players to explore (and solve your puzzles inside). Other features related to building are under consideration. We will let you know later.
    • Cooperate - One way of cooperation will be based on sharing of processed astrophotographies with other players. Processed astrophotography contain additional information as e.g. distance. Computer on your space station will “struggle” to process one astrophotography. Because of time consumption to process them you may prefer to exchange astrophotographies with other players. There will be also possibility to catalogue galaxies. Other multiplayer features are still under consideration. We will let you know later.

    Main features under consideration:

    • Time Travel!
    • Parallel universes

    I am currently working on 90Edges 0.0.7-alpha (next weekly build). Few days ago Antonin Charvat provided me the first final song (it is called Dark Matter). I have to finish sound support in 90Edges Framework to be able to use it in the next version of 90EU.

  • Now, on the beginning of development of 90Edges Universe, I am trying to use very simple and quick pseudo-random number generator (the multiply-with-carry method invented by George Marsaglia) to generate positions of stars. In this concrete case (part of universe with very high number of stars) is possible to see unwanted patterns as a result of usage of this simple pseudo-random number generator. For now it is not reason to use another pseudo-random number generator. I just have to figure out how to use it in the better way to minimalise occurence of unwanted patterns.
